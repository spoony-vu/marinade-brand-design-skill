How Marinade staking works

Four steps from SOL to earning yield across DeFi
1
Deposit SOL
Connect your wallet and deposit any amount of SOL into the Marinade staking pool
2
Receive mSOL
Get mSOL tokens representing your staked position. The exchange rate increases as rewards accrue
3
Earn yield
Staking rewards are distributed across 400+ validators using an automated delegation strategy
4
Use in DeFi
Deploy mSOL across lending, liquidity pools, and other protocols while continuing to earn staking yield
